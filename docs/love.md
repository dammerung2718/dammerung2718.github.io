# Coming Into Existence

Soon after my inception the first 4 years were already gone.
I woke up, in a bed.
People at home were celebrating a party I did not know the reason of.
Busted out of the bed in sheer terror of coming into existence,
I ran to my mother for a hug.
A woman stood beside the kitchen table, which I wasted no minute to rush into arms of.
A hug and everything was better.
I did not knew she was until I instinctively called her "mother".
I just ran in horror of the moment, and knew where to.

After that day the next year was blissful ignorance.
Like any child I fooled around, had fun without knowing it.
Broke & built many things, it was fun.
Actions without consequences and a lack of meaning.
Done purely out of curiosity.

I was confident, curious and felt safe.
It was almost perfect.


## High School

Then came the girl of my life.
I was in highschool and in dire need of attention.
My weirdness and disfunctional social skills were not helping, let alone the alcoholic father and depressed mother.
I do not blame them, but having two little sisters stole the small beacon of affection left I had and that drove me into a need.
A need to love and feel loved.

I picked an innocent, and dwelled into dreams.
I fucked up my own life.
I did not know her, we never spoke and I did not attempt either.
Later I realised it was not her I loved, it was her image in my mind I liked so much.
Years went by and I still liked her.
The pain remained.

I did not know back then, but my ability to love seized existence since that.
I no longer felt nor needed, love.
I am still very much this way.
Feelings had dissolved away, I found a way to kill them not knowing the cause and consequences.

I was confident, curious and vulnerable.
It was almost beautiful.


## University

I had mostly forgotten my pastime of dwelling into dreams and focused on reality.
It struck me fancy to discover the world and understand it.
Little victories here and there were like candy.
I did not know how poisonous candies were.

At the time I had interest in programming and decent skills.
Thought to myself, why not get a job?
So I got two.
One I had to leave due burnout.
Other due utter disgust.

The affection I needed was fulfilled by my coworkers.
They would pat on my back and say nice things.
Sometimes they would compliment my skills.

> Little they knew, I was programming to fill this gap of affection.

Once I had what I needed in, programming was out.

And I didn't know that back then either.
It came to me yesterday and I went "Aaahh so that's why".
I do not code anymore outside of work.
It is a hassle for me.
I do write tho.

I was insecure, disinterested and vulnerable.
It was utterly awful.


## Later On

I became religious.
I shall not name it, for I know faith & influence are siblings.
This helped dearly and I still pray everyday to this day.

All through that, I had this man with me

![Sergei Rachmaninoff](https://neu.edu.tr/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/27/Sergei-Rachmaninoff.jpg?ver=ea4bfa65a6724622715ad48099b7658a)

I listened to his music, read his story and spent all night thinking.
How could a man wrote such masterpieces all by himself?
Still to this day I wonder and lose sleep to this question.
A man wrote songs a literal century before my birth, and those helped me keep living.
There is a reason for my nickname, and now you know.

I am insecure, disinterested and vulnerable.
But I have hope.
