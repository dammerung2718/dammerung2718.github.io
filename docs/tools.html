Philosophy

In my defence, I have tried numerous tools. Thes ones to keep by my side have always been simpler ones. Not only are they immune to change in behaviour, they are also irreplaceable in function.

And I do not talk about grep vs ripgrep or vim vs neovim. It is their ideas and interfaces that keep them alive. The idea may take many forms but in its function the soul remains.

That’s what I take with myself everywhere, not what they embody per se, but what they stand to depict.
